# PRIVACY POLICY
Privacy Policy for HabitDot

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

HabitDot (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the HabitDot mobile application.

Information Stored on Your Device

HabitDot allows users to create habits and record daily check-ins. This information is stored locally on your device using the device’s local storage system.

HabitDot does not collect, transmit, or store your data on external servers.

Personal Information

HabitDot does not require registration, login, email address, or any personally identifiable information. All data entered into the app remains on your device unless you choose to export it.

Analytics and Tracking

HabitDot does not use analytics services, tracking technologies, advertising SDKs, or cookies. We do not monitor user behavior.

Third-Party Services

HabitDot does not integrate with third-party services that collect personal data. The app contains no advertisements and no external tracking tools.

Data Export

HabitDot allows users to export their habit data as CSV files. Exported data is controlled entirely by the user and is not transmitted by the app.

Data Deletion

Users can permanently delete all data stored by HabitDot at any time using the “Delete All Data” option available in the Settings section of the app.

Children’s Privacy

HabitDot does not collect personal information from users of any age. Because all data remains on the user’s device, the app can be used by individuals of all ages.

Security

Although HabitDot does not collect or transmit personal data, we design the app to operate securely and minimize potential vulnerabilities.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date above.
